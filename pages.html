About Restan

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1865

Our Story

Founded in 1865, Restan has been a cornerstone of culinary excellence for over 150 years. What started as a small family-owned establishment has grown into one of the most beloved restaurants in the region.

Our commitment to using only the finest ingredients, combined with traditional recipes passed down through generations, ensures that every dish we serve is a masterpiece of flavor and authenticity.

At Restan, we believe that dining is not just about food—it's about creating memorable experiences that bring people together.
